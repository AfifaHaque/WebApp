# Project Overview

## Project Title

Study Planner

## Introduction

Study Planner is a web-based application designed to help students organize their academic activities efficiently. The system enables users to manage study schedules, track assignments and deadlines, monitor progress, and maintain a structured study routine.

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to provide students with a centralized platform for planning and managing academic tasks. The application aims to improve productivity, reduce missed deadlines, and encourage effective study habits.

## Scope

The Study Planner system will provide the following features:

* User registration and login
* Task and assignment management
* Study schedule creation
* Calendar view for deadlines and events
* Progress tracking
* User profile management

## Target Users

* University students
* College students
* High school students

## Expected Benefits

* Improved time management
* Better organization of academic work
* Increased productivity
* Reduced likelihood of missing important deadlines
