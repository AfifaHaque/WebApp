# Stakeholder Analysis

## Introduction

Stakeholders are individuals or groups who have an interest in the development, operation, or success of the Study Planner system. Identifying stakeholders helps ensure that the system meets user requirements and project objectives.

## Students

Students are the primary stakeholders and intended users of the Study Planner system. They will use the application to organize academic tasks, manage study schedules, track deadlines, and monitor their progress. Their needs and feedback are the most important factors in determining the system's functionality.

## Administrators

Administrators are responsible for managing the platform and ensuring that it operates smoothly. They may oversee user accounts, monitor system activity, and maintain the overall functionality of the application.

## Development Team

The development team is responsible for gathering requirements, designing the system, implementing features, testing functionality, and maintaining the application. Their role is critical to ensuring that the system meets stakeholder expectations and project requirements.

## Project Supervisor

The project supervisor or course instructor oversees the project's progress and evaluates its documentation, design, and implementation. They provide guidance throughout the development process and ensure that software engineering practices are followed.

## Conclusion

The success of the Study Planner system depends primarily on meeting the needs of students while providing administrators with tools to manage the platform effectively. The development team and project supervisor play essential roles in ensuring that the project is completed successfully and meets its intended objectives.
