# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

Study Planner

## Product Overview

Study Planner is a web-based application designed to help students manage academic responsibilities more effectively. The application provides a centralized platform for organizing assignments, tracking deadlines, scheduling study sessions, and monitoring academic progress.

The system aims to reduce the challenges associated with managing multiple applications and scattered information by consolidating study planning tools into a single user-friendly platform.

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to keep track of assignments, examinations, and study schedules due to the use of multiple tools and fragmented workflows. This can result in missed deadlines, increased stress, and reduced productivity.

## Objectives

The Study Planner aims to:

* Help students organize academic tasks and deadlines.
* Improve time management and study planning.
* Provide reminders for important academic events.
* Reduce reliance on multiple applications.
* Encourage productive study habits.

## Target Users

The primary users of the system are university students who need a structured way to manage academic responsibilities.

## Key Features

* User registration and authentication
* Task and assignment management
* Deadline tracking
* Calendar view
* Study session scheduling
* Time-blocking support
* Progress tracking
* Reminder notifications

## Success Criteria

The product will be considered successful if:

* Students can effectively manage assignments and deadlines.
* Users report improved organization of academic tasks.
* The application provides an intuitive and user-friendly experience.
* Academic planning can be performed from a single platform.
