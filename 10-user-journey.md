# User Journey

## Scenario: Managing Academic Responsibilities

### Step 1: Registration

The student creates an account and logs into the Study Planner system.

### Step 2: Creating Academic Tasks

The student adds assignments, quizzes, examinations, and other academic responsibilities.

### Step 3: Scheduling Study Sessions

The student creates study sessions and allocates time blocks for specific courses.

### Step 4: Monitoring Deadlines

The system displays upcoming deadlines through the dashboard and calendar view.

### Step 5: Receiving Reminders

The student receives notifications for approaching deadlines and scheduled study sessions.

### Step 6: Tracking Progress

Completed tasks are marked as finished and reflected in progress statistics.

### Step 7: Maintaining Academic Organization

The student continues updating tasks and schedules throughout the semester to stay organized and productive.
