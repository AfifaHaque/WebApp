# Information Gathering

## Purpose

The purpose of information gathering was to understand the challenges students face in managing academic tasks and to identify features that would be useful in a Study Planner application.

## Research Process

Before defining the system requirements, research was conducted on common study management practices and existing productivity tools. Various applications used by students for task management and scheduling were examined to understand their features and limitations.

Applications reviewed included:

* Google Calendar
* Notion
* Microsoft To Do
* Todoist

## Findings

The research revealed that students often rely on multiple applications to manage different aspects of their academic responsibilities. While these tools provide useful functionality, they are not specifically designed for academic planning and may require students to switch between different platforms.

Common challenges identified include:

* Tracking assignment deadlines
* Managing study schedules
* Prioritizing academic tasks
* Maintaining consistent study habits

These findings helped determine the core features required for the proposed Study Planner system.

## Outcome

The information gathered during this phase was used to guide requirement analysis and feature selection for the Study Planner application.
