# Problem Statement

## Background

In today's academic environment, students are required to manage multiple responsibilities, including assignments, projects, quizzes, examinations, and extracurricular activities. As academic workloads increase, effective time management becomes essential for maintaining productivity and achieving academic success.

Many students currently rely on a combination of notebooks, calendars, reminder applications, and personal notes to keep track of their tasks and deadlines. While these tools provide partial solutions, they often lack integration and make it difficult to maintain a comprehensive overview of academic responsibilities.

## Problem Description

Students frequently encounter difficulties in organizing and prioritizing their academic tasks. Important deadlines may be overlooked, study schedules may not be followed consistently, and workload management can become overwhelming during busy academic periods.

The absence of a centralized platform specifically tailored to students' academic planning needs contributes to these challenges. Existing solutions may either be too general-purpose or require users to switch between multiple applications to manage different aspects of their studies.

## Impact of the Problem

Poor academic planning can lead to several negative outcomes, including:

* Missed assignment and project deadlines
* Inefficient use of study time
* Increased stress and anxiety
* Difficulty balancing academic and personal commitments
* Reduced academic performance

These issues affect not only students but also educational institutions that aim to support student success and engagement.

## Proposed Solution

The proposed Study Planner system will provide students with a centralized platform for managing academic activities. The system will allow users to create and organize tasks, schedule study sessions, track deadlines, monitor progress, and receive reminders for upcoming academic responsibilities.

By consolidating these functions into a single application, the Study Planner aims to improve organization, productivity, and time management among students.

## Expected Outcomes

The implementation of the Study Planner is expected to:

* Help students stay organized
* Improve time management skills
* Reduce missed deadlines
* Increase productivity
* Encourage consistent study habits
* Support better academic performance
