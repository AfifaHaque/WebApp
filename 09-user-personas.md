# User Personas

## Persona 1: Anika

### Background

Anika is a fourth-year Electrical and Electronic Engineering student. She uses note-taking applications and sticky notes to keep track of academic responsibilities.

### Goals

* Keep track of assignment deadlines.
* Avoid missing important announcements.
* Manage academic tasks efficiently.

### Pain Points

* Important notifications can get lost among other notifications.
* Academic information is spread across multiple platforms.
* Managing deadlines can become stressful during busy semesters.

### Expectations

* A simple and user-friendly interface.
* Centralized deadline tracking.
* Clear reminders for important academic events.

## Persona 2: Aoyon

### Background

Aoyon is a fourth-year Computer Science student who currently uses Focus To-Do and Google Calendar to manage his workload.

### Goals

* Organize study schedules efficiently.
* Track deadlines and examinations.
* Maintain productivity during busy academic periods.

### Pain Points

* Uses multiple applications for academic planning.
* Occasionally loses track of deadlines.
* Managing course materials can be inconvenient.

### Expectations

* Time-blocking functionality.
* Centralized task management.
* Organized file management for course materials.
